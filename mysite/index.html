About Us

TheSpaceToolkit aims at being a user-friendly arsenal of tools and services typically used by space enthusiasts.

This section contains updated details of the space launches about to happen in the future. The livestream link of these launches can also be accessed through this section.

This section consists of details about the Astronauts who are currently in space and their spacecrafts. You can also view their images by clicking on their respective names.

This section consists of live map view of the International Space Station. The location is updated every second in order to provide accurate data. This section also consists of a NASA widget to determine the upcoming visible pass of the International Space Station over your location.

This section consists of Astronomy Picture Of the Day (APOD) and Mars Picture Of the Week (MPOW). APOD provides a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe each day, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer. MPOW provides a different image or photograph of Mars caputured by Perseverance rover mission on Mars each week which is selected by public vote and featured as "Image of the Week" by NASA.

This section is used to access the subscription services provided by TheSpaceToolkit. Following services are provided currently:
1. Launch Notifier - Receive space launch details and livestream link 1 hour before launch.
2. ISS Pass Notifier - Receive email notifications with details of visible ISS pass 10 day predictions.
